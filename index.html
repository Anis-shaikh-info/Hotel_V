About Hotel Vrundavan

Nestled in the spiritual heart of Ranjangaon, Hotel Vrundavan provides a peaceful retreat for pilgrims and travelers visiting the famous Ganpati Temple. Our lodging facility is designed to offer comfort, convenience, and a memorable stay.


With our prime location at Gat No. 1570, Nagar Pune Road, we are easily accessible while providing a tranquil environment away from the hustle and bustle. Our dedicated staff ensures that your stay is comfortable and hassle-free.


Whether you're visiting for religious purposes or exploring the beauty of Maharashtra, Hotel Vrundavan is your ideal home away from home.
